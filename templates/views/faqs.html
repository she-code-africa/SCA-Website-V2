What is SCA?

SCA simply means SheCodeAfrica

What is She Code Africa?

She Code Africa is a community that is focused on celebrating and technically empowering young girls and women in technology across Africa.

Our community consists of over 1400 members around the continent and across the globe, and 12 countries presence.

What is the goal of She Code Africa?

Our goal is to have an Africa where women are equally represented across all career roles in Technology.

Our mission is to build a community that embodies technical growth, networking, mentoring and visibility among all levels and career roles in Technology.

Who are our members comprised of?

  • Developers
  • Designers
  • Data Scientists
  • Founders
  • Product
  • Others

Who is a member?

Members of She Code Africa are part of a global community of over 1400 tech professionals dedicated to supporting one another in their career goals.

Why become a member of She Code Africa?

Members of She Code Africa get access to all of our initiatives and programs that we offer to help achieve their respective career goals in each industry. Some of the benefits include:

  • Full access to coding & tech resources -Tutorials, articles, and videos.
  • Access to job opportunities and promotions to global conferences and tech events.
  • The opportunity to network and connect with like minds in the same career field.

How do I become a member?

To be a member of She Code Africa, simply sign up to join our platform here. You can also become a member by attending a local SCA chapter event close to you .

How can I get involved in She Code Africa?

You can join any She Code Africa city chapter or campus chapter nearest you. Each local community has regular events for women-in-tech to further enhance their technical skills and leadership skills. You can also follow us on Facebook, Twitter, Instagram

Can I post a job to your community?

Yes, for the meantime, you can post directly via our slack community up until our job boards feature is fully ready on our platform.

Check out our About Us page.
